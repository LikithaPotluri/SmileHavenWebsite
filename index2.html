About Dr. Nandan Kumar Singh

Dr. Nandan Kumar Singh is a skilled dentist with a passion for providing exceptional dental care. He earned his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and pursued a Master's degree in Prosthodontics from Oxford Dental College, receiving a Gold Medal for his achievements.

With years of experience, Dr. Singh specializes in prosthodontics, focusing on restoring and replacing teeth to enhance both function and appearance. He returned to Bhagalpur to provide top-notch dental services to his community, ensuring that patients receive the best possible care in a welcoming environment.
